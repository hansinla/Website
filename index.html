Television Director

About

Hans van Riet is an award winning television director with nearly three decades of experience that is as wide ranging as the markets in which he has directed. Hans started his career in the Netherlands during the birth of reality programming. Working in the rich diverse culture of the Netherlands helped shape his unique approaches to common formats. It is that background that was inspirational in the style of directing and work ethic that drives Hans’ success here in the states.

Game Shows

Whether it's the LIVE game show Opposite Worlds for Syfy Channel or the reboot of the iconic Nickelodeon game show Double Dare, Hans’ game show expertise allows for endless possibilities, ideas and creativity.

Concerts & Specials

From the first ever 3D live broadcast of the New Year’s Day family favorite, the Tournament of Roses Parade to world renowned superstars like Gloria Estefan, Lionel Richie, Julio Iglesias, and The Gipsy Kings, Hans has directed a wide variety of concerts and specials.

Unscripted

Hans’ experience in unscripted reality ranges from massive productions like the long running CBS hit show The Amazing Race for which Hans directed the inaugural season, to cable network productions like My New BFF Casting Special with Paris Hilton. Talk and Late Night also are part of the extensive experience Hans built in the U.S. and Europe.
